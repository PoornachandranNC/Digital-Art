Who We Are

DigiArt is a digital platform dedicated to showcasing the finest artwork from artists around the globe. We provide a creative space for both emerging and established artists to share their work with a broad audience. Our mission is to celebrate creativity and innovation in the digital art world, making art accessible to everyone.

Meet Our Team

Antony - Founder

A visionary digital artist who has been part of the digital art scene for over a decade, Antony founded DigiArt to create a global space for artists to showcase their work.

John - Lead Curator

John is an oil painter and curator with a keen eye for detail. His role involves handpicking and curating the best artwork for DigiArt's exhibitions.
